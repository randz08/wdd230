Wedding Receptions

We love helping young families get started as they make wedding vows in or around the temple. We strive to help both the young couple, and their families, to have the experiences of their dreams with as little stress as possible. To assist, we have a wedding reception hall available for booking, and we also offer:

  • A simple catering menu
  • Easily arranged connections with local catering companies, decorators, bakers, and photographers
  • Kitchen access to family and guest catering companies
  • Emergency backup wedding cakes
  • Assistance with decorating and setup
  • Clean-up cared for by the staff
  • Private access to fireworks (location specific)
  • Discounted honeymoon suites during extended stays
